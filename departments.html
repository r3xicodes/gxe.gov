Imperial Ministries & Regional Administration

The GXE is administered by a combination of central ministries and regional feudal authorities in the territories. Explore key ministries below and click to learn more.

Ministry of Finance

Oversees imperial treasury and taxation; manages the Empire's Xana Gold economy, budgeting, and public expenditures. Contact the Treasury office for grants and public project funding.

Contact Ministry

Ministry of Defense

Coordinates the Imperial Armed Forces, maintains national security policy, and oversees the elite Maid Corps units. Recruitment and public information are handled by regional recruitment centers.

Contact Ministry

Ministry of Health

Responsible for public health policy, disease control, and healthcare administration across the homeland and dependencies. Learn how to access services in your region.

Contact Ministry

Ministry of Colonial Affairs

Manages relations with dukes, duchesses, and colonial administrations to ensure the smooth operation of regional governance within imperial law.

Contact Ministry

National Assembly

The representative body advising on legislation and fiscal policy. The Assembly accepts petitions and public input during regular sittings.

Learn more

In outlying realms, regional rulers (dukes, lords, queens) govern in the Empress's name under feudal arrangements that respect local custom while maintaining imperial sovereignty.
