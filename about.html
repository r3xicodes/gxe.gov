About the Great Holy Xanarcica Empire

The Great Holy Xanarcica Empire (GXE), formally the Holy Alariastic Dynasty: The Great Holy Xanarcica Empire & Xana Majesty’s Territories and Realms, is a crowned imperial federation led by the Xana Crown. The Crown, embodied by Her Seraphic Majesty Empress Elaria II Xana, is both sovereign and spiritual symbol of unity.

Government

The system — known as a Xaiar Monarchy — fuses monarchy, feudal administration in colonies, republican ministries, and a National Assembly. Ministries manage civil affairs across the homeland and territories while regional dukes and lords administer local governance in the Empress’s name.

Military & Traditions

The Empire maintains a strong military tradition, centered around elite units such as the Maid Corps — a renowned force that serves both ceremonial and operational roles.
